   安装Flex SDK 4.X
2、配置ant设置环境变量
ANT_HOME   C:\Program Files\apache-ant-1.9.4
classpath  C:\Program Files\apache-ant-1.9.4\lib
Path       C:\Program Files\apache-ant-1.9.4\bin
3、设置build.properties
flexsdk = C:/Program Files/Adobe/Adobe Flash Builder 4.7/sdks/4.6.0
execextension = .exe

4、编译
ant -buildfile build\build.xml
